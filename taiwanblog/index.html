About

Hello and welcome to my blog! I’m Matt (郁志揚), a rising sophomore in Davenport College and prospective Computer Science and Music major. This summer I’ll be studying Chinese at the International Chinese Language Program in Taipei, Taiwan. As required by the Richard U. Light Fellowship, which has generously funded my summer abroad, I will be maintaining a blog over the course of my time in Taiwan. Some things this blog might include (but is not limited to): random adventures, cool photos, culture shock, unfinished thoughts, mishaps, identity-crises, ramblings, and a healthy dose of food pics. Nice!

If you have any questions or comments, or are considering studying at ICLP, please feel free to reach me at the email in the ‘Contact’ section of this page!

And lastly, one more huge thank-you to the Light Fellowship. Without their incredible support, none of this would be possible.

Thanks for visiting and I hope you enjoy the blog!
